Student Affairs Compliance Review

Review activity application compliance based on the UTM Student Activity Guidebook (2013). Each violation requires action before approval can be granted.

📖 Reference: UTM Student Activity Guidebook (2013)
🚫 Absolute Blockers (CANNOT Be Approved)
❌ Sec. 3.2.4(b) — No approval from Advisor/Principal
❌ Sec. 3.2.4(d) — Non-multiracial participation (except religious societies)
❌ Sec. 3.1.5 — Submitted less than 14 working days before programme
❌ Sec. 3.4.4 — Previous activity report not yet submitted to HEMA
⚠️ Requires Special Action
⚠️ Sec. 3.1.6 — Has external sponsorship/participation → requires 30 working days
⚠️ Sec. 3.1.9 — Exceeds 4 activities/semester → requires Advisor + HEMA approval
⚠️ Sec. 3.2.5 — During revision/exam week → requires TNC (HEMA) approval
⚠️ Sec. 3.2.10 — Activity past midnight → requires HEMA/Principal approval
⚠️ Sec. 3.2.6 — Has external invitation/sponsorship → requires Vice Chancellor approval
